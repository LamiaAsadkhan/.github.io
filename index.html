Climate Change Impact on Agricultural Productivity

Created By

Project Overview

This interactive dashboard visualizes the impact of climate change on agricultural productivity metrics across regions over time. It allows users to explore patterns, trends, and correlations between climatic factors and crop yield variations.

Dataset Used

The dataset includes climate variables (temperature, rainfall, etc.) and agricultural output data. It helps in analyzing how shifts in weather patterns influence productivity.
